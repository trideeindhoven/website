WHY 2025 - Knock knock who's there 2.0, the subtle art of (physical) port knocking

2025/08/11

Jeroen Hermans

Translations of this article:

At WHY2025 i gave a talk about security of building entrance systems

Building entrance systems are important and often the first layer of security for your facility. At MCH2022 i gave a talk “Knock Knock Who’s There” and that received really positive reviews.
During the next few years i researched a few more of these systems and that led to the Knock Knock 2.0 presentation during WHY2025. You can see the video below and the full text below that.
