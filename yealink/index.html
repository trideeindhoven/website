Yealink

This website describes how i got involved in researching the security of products of the Chinese telecommunication manufacturer Yealink. This research led to many interesting events and it was eventually published in the a number of international media, such as:

  • Follow The Money (NL/EN)
  • L’echo (FR)
  • De Tijd (BE)
  • Le Vif (FR)
  • Le Soir (FR)

During the research conducted by myself and journalists Siem Eikelenboom and Sebastiaan Brommersma numerous technical security issues were uncovered. But it also led to the discovery of an economic crime by one of the largest European publically traded corporations. Because the dataset that resulted from the research was that extensive it was decided a public accessible dataroom was needed. This dataroom is what you are looking at. It is a constantly updated website where sourcematerial is presented with background information and links to other pieces of information.
